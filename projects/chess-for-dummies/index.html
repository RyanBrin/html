Chess For Dummies

What is Chess?

Chess is a multi-piece game were two players will make moves on a checker-pattern board while trying to capture the other player pieces. A player wins in Chess by delivering checks to the oposing players King in order to checkmate their king. A checkmate is check were the King is unable to move to any safe squares to remove the check resulting in a checkmate, with the other player winning from this.

Openings

Openings are one of the biggest elements in Chess as in Chess it is veryimportant to your success in the later game. Openings allow you to develope your pieces and form attacks on the other playes pieces in order to gain a material advantage over them which also fuels success in the late game.

How to get better

Chess is one of those things that feels impossible to improve on, but its really not! Chess takes time and experience to get better for some people, but many find that playing higher elo players will also improve your Chess skills. If this does not work for you then playing regular Chess games with someone is also very good. If you have no friends thats okay! Sites like Chess.com exist for the sole reason of helping all types of players improve or learn the game.
